Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2026

Indie Studio ("we", "our", or "us") operates Kuratd (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

Information You Provide

When you use Kuratd, you may provide:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address for authentication purposes.
  • Podcast Subscriptions: The RSS feed URLs of podcasts you subscribe to.
  • Interest Profiles: Topics and preferences you configure to personalize your experience.

Automatically Collected Information

The App may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage statistics (features used, time spent)
  • Crash reports and performance data

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To personalize your podcast briefs based on your interests
  • To improve the App through usage analytics
  • To send important updates about the App (if you opt-in)
  • To provide customer support

Data Processing

On-Device Processing

Kuratd prioritizes your privacy by performing transcription locally on your device using WhisperKit. Audio files are processed on-device and are not sent to our servers.

Cloud AI Services

To generate AI-powered summaries and briefs, transcript text is sent to our secure cloud service which uses OpenAI's API. This data is:

  • Transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption
  • Not stored permanently on our servers
  • Not used to train AI models
  • Processed in accordance with OpenAI's usage policies

Data Storage

Your data is stored as follows:

  • Local Storage: Transcripts, briefs, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Cloud Sync (Optional): If you enable cloud sync, your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with encryption at rest.
  • Account Data: Authentication data is managed securely through Supabase Auth.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With service providers who assist in operating the App (subject to confidentiality agreements)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Export: Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and cloud features

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@kuratd.com.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can delete your account and associated data at any time through the App's settings.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure credential storage using iOS Keychain
  • Encryption at rest for cloud-stored data
  • Regular security audits and updates

Children's Privacy

Kuratd is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@kuratd.com
  • Website: kuratd.com/support
