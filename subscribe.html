Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as an episode credit?

Each podcast episode you process uses one credit, regardless of length. Credits reset monthly on your billing date.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

Why subscribe on the web vs. in-app?

Subscribing on the web helps us keep prices low by avoiding app store fees. You get the same features either way.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay through Stripe's secure payment system.
