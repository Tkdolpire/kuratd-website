Download the app and complete the setup wizard. You'll enter your OpenAI API key and download a Whisper transcription model. Then add your favorite podcast feeds and Kuratd will automatically process new episodes into actionable intelligence briefs.
Kuratd uses OpenAI's GPT models to generate intelligent summaries, extract key insights, and identify important metrics from podcast transcripts. You'll need your own API key from OpenAI. Processing typically costs less than $0.01 per episode.
Yes. Audio transcription happens entirely on your device using WhisperKit. Only the transcript text is sent to OpenAI for summarization. We never store, access, or share your podcast content or personal data.
Start with the Base model for faster processing. If you need better accuracy for complex audio or non-English content, try Medium or Large. Larger models require more disk space and processing time but provide better results.
Kuratd lets you share insights via Twitter/X threads, LinkedIn posts, and HTML newsletters. You can also copy content to your clipboard to paste anywhere. Weekly digests can be shared as formatted social posts with one click.
