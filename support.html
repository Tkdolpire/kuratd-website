Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Kuratd?

Download the app and complete the setup wizard. You'll enter your OpenAI API key and download a Whisper transcription model. Then add your favorite podcast feeds and Kuratd will automatically process new episodes into actionable intelligence briefs.

Why do I need an OpenAI API key?

Kuratd uses OpenAI's GPT models to generate intelligent summaries, extract key insights, and identify important metrics from podcast transcripts. You'll need your own API key from OpenAI. Processing typically costs less than $0.01 per episode.

How does transcription work?

Kuratd uses WhisperKit for on-device transcription, meaning your audio is processed locally on your device and never sent to external servers. This ensures maximum privacy. When available, we also detect and use official transcripts provided by podcast publishers.

Is my data private?

Yes. Audio transcription happens entirely on your device using WhisperKit. Only the transcript text is sent to OpenAI for summarization. We never store, access, or share your podcast content or personal data.

Which Whisper model should I choose?

Start with the Base model for faster processing. If you need better accuracy for complex audio or non-English content, try Medium or Large. Larger models require more disk space and processing time but provide better results.

How do I share my podcast insights?

Kuratd lets you share insights via Twitter/X threads, LinkedIn posts, and HTML newsletters. You can also copy content to your clipboard to paste anywhere. Weekly digests can be shared as formatted social posts with one click.

Why isn't my podcast being processed?

Check that you've downloaded a Whisper model in Settings and entered a valid OpenAI API key. Also ensure you have a stable internet connection. If processing stalls, try restarting the app or re-downloading the Whisper model.

How do I manage my subscription?

If you subscribed on the web, manage your subscription through your Stripe account. If you subscribed through the App Store, go to Settings on your device, tap your Apple ID, then Subscriptions to manage or cancel.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and all associated data from Settings within the app. If you need assistance, contact us at tyron@indiestudio.ai.

System Requirements

  • macOS: Mac running macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later
  • iOS: iPhone or iPad running iOS 17.0 or later
  • Storage: At least 2GB free space (for Whisper models)
  • Internet: Required for API calls and model downloads
