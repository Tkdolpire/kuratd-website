Frequently Asked Questions

How does transcription work?

Kuratd uses WhisperKit for on-device transcription, meaning your audio is processed locally on your device and never sent to external servers. This ensures maximum privacy. When available, we also detect and use official transcripts provided by podcast publishers.

What AI is used for summaries?

We use OpenAI's GPT models to generate intelligent summaries and extract key insights from transcripts. Only the text transcript is sent to OpenAI - never the original audio. Your data is not used to train AI models.

How do I add podcasts?

You can add podcasts by searching for them in the app or by pasting an RSS feed URL directly. Kuratd automatically monitors your feeds for new episodes.

What are intelligence profiles?

Intelligence profiles let you customize what information Kuratd extracts from podcasts. You can set topics of interest, keywords to watch for, and the types of insights you want to capture (quotes, action items, metrics, etc.).

Is my data backed up?

By default, all data is stored locally on your device. If you create an account, you can optionally enable cloud sync to back up your data securely to our servers.

How do I export my data?

Kuratd supports multiple export formats including Markdown, PDF, and integrations with Obsidian and Notion. You can export individual episodes or batch export your entire library.

What devices are supported?

Kuratd is available for iOS (iPhone and iPad) running iOS 17.0 or later, and macOS running macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions on your device to manage or cancel your subscription.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and all associated data from Settings within the app. If you need assistance, contact us at privacy@kuratd.com.

System Requirements

  • iOS: iPhone or iPad running iOS 17.0 or later
  • macOS: Mac running macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later
  • Storage: At least 2GB free space (for Whisper models)
  • Internet: Required for AI summarization and cloud features
